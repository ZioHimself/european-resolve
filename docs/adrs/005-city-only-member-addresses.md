# ADR-005: City-Only Member Addresses on Public Site

**Status:** Accepted
**Date:** 2026-05-09

## Problem

Digital business cards need location information. Full addresses are available from the organisation's statute (public Belgian registry), but the team works on European security — making them potential targets.

## Decision

Show city only (e.g., "Antwerp", "Brussels"). No street addresses on the public website.

## Rationale

- Information security policy states: "We work on European security. That makes us a target."
- Public registry filings and a well-indexed, SEO-optimized website have different threat profiles
- City provides enough context for a business card without enabling physical targeting
- Full addresses remain in the statute for legal compliance

## Consequences

- Business cards are less detailed than traditional ones — acceptable for a security-conscious org
- If a member wants their full address shown, it can be added as an opt-in per-member field
